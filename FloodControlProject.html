Project Overview

This website explores the political and civic issues surrounding flood control projects in the Philippines. It highlights allegations of corruption, lack of environmental compliance, poor planning, and consequences experienced by Filipino communities. The goal is to present a clear analysis supported by organized information and integrated multimedia to raise awareness and encourage informed civic engagement.

Key Issues

Lack of Master Plan

Flood-control initiatives are often uncoordinated, leading to inefficiency and potentially worsening conditions in other areas.

Environmental Concerns

Many projects lack proper Environmental Compliance Certificates (ECC), resulting in harmful interventions to waterways.

Corruption & Misuse of Funds

Reports reveal ghost projects, overpriced contracts, and substandard work, raising concerns on accountability.
